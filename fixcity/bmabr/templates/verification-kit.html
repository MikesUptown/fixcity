Verify in two easy steps:

  1. Confirm the location is appropriate for a rack. Adjust address if needed--open space may be just around the corner.
  2. Make sure suggestion has a good photo of where the rack should go.

Print the rack placement guide to take out with you. Statement of Support is only required for residential buildings and schools. And remember, your arm-span is as wide as you are tall, so you can measure with your own body or bike!
